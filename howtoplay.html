Highcarder - How to Play

Highcarder is a roguelike poker-inspired deckbuilder card game where the main goal is to earn as high of a score as possible through playing Poker Hands with an ever-dwindling deck of playing cards—earning packs, perks, and upgrades for your deck along the way—whilst trying not to run out of cards.

The Basics

In Highcarder, your main aim is to survive as many "antes" as possible without running out of cards.

  • You start with a basic 52 card deck and 7 cards in your hand.
  • There are 5 rounds in every ante. You progress each round by playing a poker hand.
  • After every ante, you are able to upgrade your deck if your score is above the "Upgrade Threshold"
  • Exceeding the "Upgrade Threshold" allows you choose multiple upgrades—one for each multiple of the threshold. For example, if your score is 300 and the threshold is 300, you can choose 10 upgrades (3000 ÷ 300 = 10)
  • The upgrade threshold will increase every ante.
  • You cannot progress onto the next ante until you meet the threshold.
  • Every five antes, the difficulty will increase and a "debuff" will be added.
  • There is no win condition, only a lose condition: Run out of cards to play.
